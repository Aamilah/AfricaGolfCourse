What makes this course special.

Designed By

Renowned golf champion Bernhard Langer, who crafted a course that harmoniously blends with the island's natural beauty.

Established In

2003

Course Type

Coastal. Set on a private island with spectacular ocean views, featuring narrow fairways, water hazards, and undulating greens, blending seaside beauty with strategic play

Tee Options

  • Langer Tees: 7,115 yards (6,469 meters)
  • Championship Tees: 6,500 yards (5,943 meters)
  • Tournament Tees: 5,992 yards (5,448 meters)
  • Forward Tees: 5,200 yards (4,724 meters)
